What is Node

High-tech startup creating new benchmark of wireless energy transmission for any device inside/outside homes and offices. In the consumer sector this technology is actively used to charge smartphones, tablets, wearable devices and other electronics. Node tries to develope it to a wide range of applications in consumer electronics, automotive, medical and many other industries. Our investors get an opportunity not only to increase their capital, but also to become the first owners of such devices

  • Intelligent

    Transfer energy at the distance to many devices with any power at the same time

  • Responsive

    Technology can be applied to any electronics

  • Reasonable

    Low to medium cost, in comparison with other solutions, energy conversion efficiency (90%)

  • Smart Design

    Less wires and more space saving. Convenient use and storage

  • Stylish

    Ergonomic and modern design
